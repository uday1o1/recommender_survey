Welcome! 👋
DBMS and Data Engineering Job/Internship Recommender System
Why this matters:
  • Finding the right DBMS role is hard — most people apply broadly and hear nothing back
  • Your responses help build a smarter matching system that connects people to roles that actually fit their skills and goals
  • The dataset powers research that could benefit the entire DBMS and related job-seeking community
  • Results from this research will be shared publicly
Goal: Build a dataset to design and evaluate a recommender system for Data Engineering & DBMS job matching, powering a RecSys research project.

What makes this unique: Your skill ratings create a person × skill × opportunity tripartite graph spanning databases, pipelines, cloud platforms, and analytics — enabling novel RS research in a high-demand career domain.

Time: ~10 minutes  |  Privacy: Responses are fully anonymized. No personally identifiable information is collected unless you choose to provide your email at the end.
