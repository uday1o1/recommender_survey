Welcome! 👋
AI Career Opportunities Survey — Help us build smarter job matching for everyone in AI
Why this matters:
  • Finding the right AI role is hard — most people apply broadly and hear nothing back
  • Your responses help build a smarter matching system that connects people to roles that actually fit their skills and goals
  • The dataset powers research that could benefit the entire AI job-seeking community
  • Results from this research will be shared publicly
Goal: Build a dataset to design and evaluate AI job recommender systems as part of an ongoing research project.

What makes this unique: Your skill ratings form a person x skill x opportunity tripartite graph enabling novel recommender systems research.

Time: ~10 minutes  |  Privacy: Responses are fully anonymized. No personally identifiable information is collected unless you choose to provide your email at the end.
